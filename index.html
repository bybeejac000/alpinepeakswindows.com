Exterior Cleanings

Exterior cleanings are the most popular among our customers. We service all houses of any sizes and can reach any high windows you may have with our unique water purifying system. We make sure those windows are spotless and streakless so they look great after those spring rain storms. Typical rates for 1-story houses start at just $115 and just $145 for 2-story houses, some of the cheapest rates you will find in the Utah Valley. Call today to get your appointment booked!

Hard Water Removal

You know those stubborn hard water stains that are everywhere in Utah and seem impossible to remove? We can take care of those for you! Nobody likes to look through a misty window that no amount of scrubbing can clear, with our special removal formula we make it so those windows are crystal clear again!

Screen Repair

Do you have any holes in your screens? Or maybe some worn out thread-bare ones? We take care of those for you, and make them look brand new to keep those bugs out of your house all summer long! At the low cost of $15/screen and $30/screen door we can fix any type or size. Give us a call or send us a text so we can get those taken care of for you!
